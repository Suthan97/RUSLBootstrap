the dawn of human civilisation, from astrology to simple household functions, the art and the science of extracting, processing and obtaining useful insights from structured and unstructured sources of data has been entangled with human existence.

This early day technique has now developed into a science called “Data Science” which is massively used in the modern world due to rapid development in computational, information and communication technology.

This (what do you mean?), an art in one aspect and a science in another, has been later coined as “Data Science” and has gained much popularity in the modern world due to the recent exponential advancements in the computational, information and communication technologies.

To date, Sri Lanka has only touched the mere shallow waters in the paradigm of data science, and hence the higher education institutions of Sri Lanka have to be more sensitive to the modern day work demand, and meet the expected international standards of a graduate. For which it’s salient to provide in-depth learning and industrial learning and researching platforms conducted in-house.

To date, Sri Lanka has only touched the mere shallow waters in the paradigm of data science and hence, still lacks in-depth academic as well as industrial research and development conducted in-house.

With the recent uprising of rapid development of the country, it is prudent to establish a platform which can mark its presence globally and locally.

International Centre for data analytics and computing at the Faculty of Applied Sciences, Rajarata University of Sri Lanka is established as one of the hallmark platforms to take Sri Lanka to the next phase of research and development through multidisciplinary data science research and development. This centre is launching its research projects, with an intention to reach international platforms in years to come. The quality of a product -tangible or intangible- are always considered important , Therefore, the centre focuses on mould researches and industrialists that are of high calibre who promote and carry out high quality research fostering linkages between local and international industries.


Success of a research centre always uses failure and constant learning to uplift from where it stands. We, as a team, envision the possible future crisis and complications that might occur and do research,collect data and analyse them and to devise a solution to avoid or minimize such a crisis.
